About Me

I am a PhD student working in theoretical particle physics. My research focuses on baryon electromagnetic form factors, hyperon production, charmonium decays into baryon–antibaryon pairs, and dispersion-relations methods.

Research Interests

  • Baryon electromagnetic form factors
  • Hyperon production in electron–positron annihilation
  • Charmonium decays into baryon–antibaryon pairs
  • Dispersion relations and analyticity
  • QCD phenomenology
  • Hadronic final-state interactions
