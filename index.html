About Me

I am a PhD student in Physics at the University of Perugia. My research focuses on the study of baryon electromagnetic form factors in the time-like region, hadronic structure, charmonium decays into baryon-antibaryon pairs, and dispersion-theoretical approaches to strong interactions.

Research

My current research is centered on baryon form factors and their connection to hadron structure, analyticity, dispersion relations and experimental measurements from modern particle physics facilities.

I am also interested in charmonium decays, QCD phenomenology, hadronic final-state interactions, and the interplay between strong and electromagnetic amplitudes.
